Eggless/Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home Recipes Poetry Contact

~ The most soft and delicious goodness of a homemade chocolate chip cookie without any guilt. ~

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 12-15 minutes

Total Time: 27-30 minutes

Servings: 10-12

Ingredients:

1. 1/2 Cup White Sugar

2. 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar Packed

3. 1 tsp salt

4. 1/2 Cup Butter or Vegan Butter or Coconut Oil

5. 1/4 Cup Milk (Dairy or Non-Dairy Refrence)

6. 1 tsp vanilla extract

7. 1 1/2 Cups all-purpose flour

8. 1/2 tsp baking soda

9. 8 oz of mix of semi-sweet chocolate chunks and dark chocolate chunks(Vegan chocolate chunks, if necessary)

Recipe:

1. In a bowl, mix together white sugar, brown sugar and salt. Then add melted butter or vegan butter or coconut oil until one homogenous batter.

2. Next, add a dairy or non-dairy milk and vanilla extract to the batter until smooth. Also, preheat your oven to 350 degrees F or 180 degrees C.

3. Then, sift through all-purpose flour and baking soda and add to the mix. Using a spatula fold everything in the mixture.

4. Then add chocolate chunks to the cookie mix.

5. Cool the dough in the refrigator for at least half n hour. This helps hold the dough together but it can be skipped.

6. Using an icecream scoop, scoop the dough onto a greased or parchment lined baking sheet. Leave at least 2 inches of space between cookies to allow for an even spread.

7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown. Then take them out and the cookies are just out of the oven they have a tendency to break, so to avoid that cool them completely.

8. ENJOY! with milk.
